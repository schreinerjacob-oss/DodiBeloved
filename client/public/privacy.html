Privacy Policy — dodi

dodi (“we”, “the app”) is designed so that your data stays on your device and is not shared with third parties for advertising or profiling.

Data we store

All message, memory, and account data is stored locally on your device and encrypted using keys derived from your passphrase. We do not have access to your messages or media.

Signaling (PeerJS)

To connect you with your partner, the app uses a signaling service (PeerJS, 0.peerjs.com). That service may see your IP address and a temporary peer ID only for the purpose of establishing a direct connection. It does not store your messages or content.

Push notifications

If you enable notifications, we use your device’s push token only to deliver “new message” or “incoming call” alerts. Notification content is minimal; message content is not sent to our servers.

No selling of data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the address provided in the app or on the store listing.
